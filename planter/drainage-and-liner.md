# Drainage and Liner

## Status: planned / in progress

Geotextile liner installation is underway but not yet complete or
documented in detail. This file will be filled in with the actual
approach once it's built, rather than describing an idealized design.

## Intent

- A geotextile liner is planned to separate soil from the wooden box
  structure, to reduce moisture contact with the wood and to keep soil
  from washing out through any drainage gaps.
- Drainage path for excess irrigation/rain water is not yet finalized.

## Open questions
